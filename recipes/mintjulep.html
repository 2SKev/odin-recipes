Mint Julep

Description

A smooth and refresh icy cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 60ml Bulleit Bourbon
  • 2 tea-spoon Sugar
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Crushed ice

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and mint in the bottom of a glass and stir to dissolve sugar.
  2. FIll glass with Bulleit Bourbon and Crushed ice and stir to chill and dilute.
  3. Garnish with a mint sprig and straw.
