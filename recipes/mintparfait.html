Chocolate Mint Parfait

Description

Mint Parfait is the perfect dessert for a sunny day, bringing the refreshment of a parfait, combinned with the wonderful flavor of mint and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 (3.9-ounce) package chocolate instant pudding and pie filling mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint extract
  • 1/4 cup crème de menthe chocolate candy thin, chopped
  • Mint leaves or crème de menthe chocolate candy

Steps

  1. Combine milk, pudding mix and mint extract in bowl. Prepare according to package directions. Cover; refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
  2. Spray generous dollop of whipped cream into each of 4 stemmed dessert glasses. Top each with 1 teaspoon candy and 1/4 cup pudding. Repeat layers.
  3. Garnish with mint leaves or candy.
